About

Synergy Tech

This is a premier tech event organized by the Delhi Tech Circuit bringing together tech clubs across Delhi NCR for innovation, competition, and collaboration.

Event Highlights


Hackathon

Join us for a thrilling 24 to 48-hour hackathon where teams will collaborate to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. Participants will have the chance to showcase their coding skills and creativity while competing for exciting prizes. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a newcomer, this is a perfect opportunity to learn, network, and create!


Networking Lounge

The Networking Lounge is the perfect space for attendees to connect, collaborate, and share ideas. Engage in meaningful conversations with fellow participants, industry professionals, and potential collaborators. Whether you're looking for a team for the hackathon or want to discuss project ideas, this is the place to be!


Startup Pitch Competition

Aspiring entrepreneurs are invited to pitch their startup ideas to a panel of judges and industry veterans. Participants will receive constructive feedback, networking opportunities, and the chance to win valuable prizes. Whether you're at the ideation stage or have a developed concept, this is an excellent platform to showcase your innovation!


Career Fair

The Career Fair connects students with top tech companies seeking interns and entry-level talent. Attendees can meet with recruiters, learn about available positions, and gain insights into different career paths in the tech industry. Bring your resumes and prepare for exciting opportunities!


Gaming Tournament

Get ready for an action-packed Gaming Tournament where participants will compete in popular video games for glory and prizes. Join in on the fun and test your skills against fellow gamers. Spectators are welcome to cheer on their friends and enjoy the electrifying atmosphere!
